I have gotten a lot of experience in graphic design from my music and other things I have designed for fun, such as user interfaces. I combine graphic design with motion and also create vibrant visuals.

Design With Motion

This is an example of a mix that I made with a pretty aesthetic cover and minimal motion. On the square cover, I had it be a little more chaotic, but still aesthetic and pushed to the background, and I still made sure that everything had room to breathe.

On this one, I decided to add in some motion with the background shaking, and have the logo respond to the bass. I also decided to have the background be symmetrical, which I felt worked best with this style. The most challenging part of making this was actually in making sure that the colors on the logo blended well with everything else.

This one's a bit older, but I decided to have more motion with the background, and have the background be a looped video, in which I also chose for it to be a more minimalistic background video. I feel like this gives early to mid 2010s vibes, which is also how this song is.
